About

The consultancy is run by John Bargh who qualified as a Chartered Building Surveyor in 1981. He has experience dealing with maintenance, conservation and production of reports associated with traditional and historic buildings. He holds a MSc in Architectural Conservation and is a full member of the Institute of Historic Building Conservation. The consultancy is based in Cumbria but will undertake commissions throughout the United Kingdom.

During his career John was project manager for a stonework restoration and underpinning contract at the Grade ll* Listed King Edward l Monument at Burgh by Sands near Carlisle. His role as project manager also included a large refurbishment contract at the Grade ll Listed Educational Settlement in Maryport. He has undertaken numerous building surveys of old and listed properties including the historic Citadel in Carlisle, historic properties owned by Leonard Cheshire Disability and Roman Catholic Churches.
